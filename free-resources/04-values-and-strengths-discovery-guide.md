## Why This Matters

Your values determine what makes work meaningful. Your strengths determine where you excel effortlessly. When your career aligns with both, work feels fulfilling rather than draining.

**Time needed:** 45-60 minutes

## Part 2: Discover Your Strengths

### What is a Strength?

A strength isn't just something you're good at—it's something that:
✓ You're naturally good at
✓ Energises you when you use it
✓ Others recognise in you
✓ You want to develop further
