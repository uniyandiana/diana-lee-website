## About This Assessment

Not everyone is ready to start a business‚Äîand that's okay. This assessment helps you honestly evaluate where you are and what you need before taking the leap.

**Time required:** 20-30 minutes

**Output:** A clear picture of your entrepreneurial readiness across 6 key dimensions

---

## How to Use This Assessment

For each statement, rate yourself honestly:

- **1** = Strongly disagree
- **2** = Disagree
- **3** = Neutral
- **4** = Agree
- **5** = Strongly agree

Be brutally honest. This isn't about what you wish were true‚Äîit's about where you actually are right now.

## Section-by-Section Analysis

**Review your lowest-scoring section. That's where to focus.**

### If "Problem & Solution Clarity" is lowest:
‚ûú Conduct 15-20 customer interviews
‚ûú Refine your problem statement
‚ûú Test your solution hypothesis

### If "Market Understanding" is lowest:
‚ûú Research competitors thoroughly
‚ûú Understand pricing and business models in your space
‚ûú Identify your unique differentiation

### If "Skills & Resources" is lowest:
‚ûú Take courses or find co-founders to fill skill gaps
‚ûú Build a financial runway
‚ûú Connect with mentors and advisors

### If "Mindset & Resilience" is lowest:
‚ûú Consider whether entrepreneurship aligns with your personality
‚ûú Build resilience through smaller challenges first
‚ûú Work with a coach or therapist on mindset

### If "Commitment & Risk Tolerance" is lowest:
‚ûú Evaluate whether the timing is right
‚ûú Consider starting as a side project first
‚ûú Have honest conversations with stakeholders

### If "Business Model Clarity" is lowest:
‚ûú Map out how you'll make money
‚ûú Run financial projections
‚ûú Understand unit economics
