# Career Clarity Workbook
### A Psychology-Informed Guide to Finding Your Direction

*Created by Diana Lee | Career Development Facilitator*

## How to Use This Workbook

This workbook guides you through structured self-reflection to gain career clarity. Set aside 60-90 minutes in a quiet space. Be honest—there are no "right" answers.

**You'll need:**
- A notebook or digital doc for expanded answers
- Uninterrupted time
- An open, curious mindset
